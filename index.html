About Us

Family owned and operated in Warrensburg, MO

Turnbow Livestock is located on the Turnbow Century Farm in Warrensburg, Johnson County, Missouri. Our farm dates back to 1889 and has included seven generations. Feeding out steers started as a hobby for our family in 2015, and swiftly became a large passion in our lives as the demand for our beef grew.

At Turnbow Livestock, our cattle and pigs are part of the family, too. They are born and pasture-raised on our land with no antibiotics, hormones, or steroids. Our cattle are pasture-raised on 80 acres of high quality grassland, well water, and fed daily with a custom corn mixture. Our cattle and pigs are raised and marketed locally, requiring less transportation to lessen the impact on the environment.

WAYS TO PURCHASE TURNBOW BEEF OR PORK

Look for us at the summer farmers' market in Warrensburg, MO and Knob Noster, MO. Special promotions offered by our friends at the Green Truck Bakery are available on Facebook. We offer free LOCAL delivery during the winter months (October thru April). Call or Email throughout the year for current pricing and cut availability.

Our products

From our farm to your table, Turnbow Livestock offers a wide range of items such as ground beef patties, steaks, briskets, and rotating specials on market days. We also offer the option to purchase a quarter or a half beef! Popular pork options include breakfast sausage, bratwursts, and bone-in chops. Most importantly, all of our beef and pork is corn-fed, non-antibiotic, and USDA inspected.
